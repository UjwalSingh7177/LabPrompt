About LabPrompt

At LabPrompt Infraprojects LLP, we specialize in turnkey solutions that bring cleanrooms, labs, and healthcare facilities to life. From design and engineering to manufacturing and installation, we deliver world-class infrastructure built for safety, compliance, and innovation. With our Mumbai-based manufacturing hub and expert team, we partner with leading organizations in pharma, healthcare, biotechnology, research, and commercial sectors to create smarter, future-ready spaces.

Why Us

We deliver turnkey solutions with unmatched expertise, blending design, engineering, and execution under one roof. Our commitment to quality, compliance, and on-time delivery makes us the trusted partner for pharma, healthcare, biotechnology, and commercial sectors.

Our History

Founded in Mumbai, LabPrompt Infraprojects LLP has grown from a focused infrastructure provider into a leading turnkey project specialist. With years of experience, we’ve successfully delivered world-class labs, cleanrooms, and healthcare facilities nationwide.

Pioneer

As pioneers in turnkey infrastructure, we combine innovation, precision, sustainability, and excellence to create future-ready spaces. From advanced cleanrooms to complex healthcare and commercial projects, we lead with solutions that redefine industry standards and empower progress.

FAQ

+

What types of products do you manufacture?

We specialize in Laboratory Furniture, Fume Hoods, Chemical Storage Cabinets, Eco-friendly Scrubbers, Spot Extractors, Hospital Furniture, Stainless Steel & Cleanroom Furniture, along with turnkey laboratory and industrial setups.

+

Do you provide turnkey lab and hospital setups?

Yes, we are specialists in turnkey projects. From lab layout design to complete execution, including cleanrooms and operation theatres, we handle end-to-end solutions tailored to client requirements.

+

Do you offer custom lab setups?

Absolutely. We offer customized solutions for different laboratory environments, ensuring functionality, safety, and compliance with international standards. Our team works closely with clients to design layouts that fit their specific needs.

+

What are some of your notable projects?

We have successfully completed projects such as setting up the Swine Flu Research Lab at Serum Institute, turnkey setup for Seven Hills Hospitals, and numerous startup labs supporting scientists becoming entrepreneurs. We also received the Business Excellence Award in 2010.

+

Do you serve clients outside Mumbai?

Our manufacturing setup is based in Mumbai, India. However, we serve clients across the country and also support international projects depending on requirements.

How can I request a quote?

You can reach out to us through our website or contact our office directly. Share your requirements, and our team will provide a tailored proposal with specifications, layout options, and cost estimates.
